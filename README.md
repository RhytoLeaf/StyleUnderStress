# Style Under Stress: The Quiz

**Discover your communication style in high-stakes conversations.**

This self-assessment tool helps you understand how you react under pressure. Do you tend to shut down (silence) or become more forceful (violence)? Knowing your style is the first step to improving your communication and achieving better outcomes.

## What is "Style Under Stress"?

Based on the concepts from the book "Crucial Conversations," your "Style Under Stress" is your default communication approach when stakes are high. This quiz helps you identify your tendencies, so you can make more conscious and effective choices in your most important conversations.

## Why Take the Quiz?

*   **Gain Self-Awareness:** Understand your automatic reactions in stressful situations.
*   **Improve Relationships:** Learn how your communication style impacts others and how to foster more productive dialogue.
*   **Achieve Better Outcomes:** Develop the skills to navigate difficult conversations with confidence and achieve your goals.
*   **Personal Growth:** Identify areas for improvement and start your journey towards more effective communication.

## Features

*   **33-Question Assessment:** A comprehensive quiz to accurately identify your style.
*   **Instant Results:** Receive immediate feedback on your tendencies towards silence or violence.
*   **Detailed Breakdown:** Get a closer look at your specific behaviors within each style.
*   **Actionable Insights:** The results provide a foundation for you to start improving your communication skills.

## How to Get Started

1.  Open the `index.html` file in your web browser.
2.  Click the "Begin" button to start the quiz.
3.  Answer the 33 questions honestly based on your typical behavior.
4.  Review your results and reflect on your communication style.
